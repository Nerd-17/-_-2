Tribute to Macarena Achaga

Macarena Achaga Figueroa, known professionally as Macarena Achaga, is an Argentine model, actress, singer, and television hostess. In 2012, she debuted as an actress on the Mexican television series Miss XV and was a member of the Mexican-Argentine pop group, Eme 15, from 2011 to 2014.

About Macarena Achaga

Macarena Achaga Figueroa is an Argentine model, actress, singer, and television hostess. Known for her versatile career, Macarena has captivated audiences in both Latin America and internationally. Her breakout role came with the hit television series Miss XV, which was followed by her stellar performances in shows like La Piloto and Amar a Muerte. With her unique blend of talent and charisma, she continues to be a beloved figure in the entertainment industry.
